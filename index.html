Nothing you type or design is collected, tracked, or sent to any server. There's no account, no analytics, no cookies.

Your work in progress — the print queue — is saved locally in this browser so it's still here next time you visit. It never leaves your device, and clearing your browser data (or switching browser or device) starts you fresh.

The only outside requests the page makes are to Google Fonts (for the typefaces) and, when you search for an icon, to the Iconify icon API. No personal data is sent in either.
